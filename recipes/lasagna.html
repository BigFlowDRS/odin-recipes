Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna is the ultimate comfort food. Layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, and gooey cheese make this dish a family favorite. It is perfect for Sunday dinner or for freezing to enjoy later.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 1 box lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 lb mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C) and lightly grease a 9x13 baking dish.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles in a large pot of boiling water until al dente, then drain.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain fat and stir in the marinara sauce.
  4. In a small bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, and parmesan cheese together.
  5. Build the layers: Spread a little meat sauce on the bottom of the pan. Top with noodles, then the ricotta mixture, then mozzarella. Repeat layers.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 15 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  7. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
